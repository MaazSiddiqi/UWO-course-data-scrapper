APPLMATH 2811B - LINEAR ALGEBRA II

Course Description: Vector space examples. Inner products, orthogonal sets including Legendre polynomials, trigonometric functions, wavelets. Coupled difference and differential equations; applications such as predator-prey, business competition, coupled oscillators. Singular value decomposition, image approximations. Linear transformations, graphics. Extra Information: 3 lecture hours.
