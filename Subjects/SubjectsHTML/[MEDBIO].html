MEDBIO 4970E - RESEARCH PROJECT IN BIOPHYSIC

Course Description: Major laboratory course in experimental biophysics for Honours Specialization modules offered by the Department of Medical Biophysics. Three components are: a major experimental project (topic and advisor chosen in consultation with the student), scientific communication (student presentation and reports), and electronic information processing (data capture, computer analysis of biophysical signals). Antirequisite(s): Medical Biophysics 4971E. Extra Information: 15 hours weekly on average.
